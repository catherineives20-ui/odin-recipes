Greek Salad Recipe

Description

Tasty Greek Salad Highlights Tomato, Feta and Olives.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Cherry Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Feta Cheese
  • Red Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Dice Cucumer into Small Quarters
  2. Slice Cherry Tomatoes in Half
  3. Cut Red Onion in Half and Slice
  4. Dice Feta Cheese into Small Cubes
  5. Chop Lettuce
  6. Combine these Ingredients into a Large Bowl
  7. Add Olives, Olive Oil and Oregano
  8. Mix Well and Serve
