Marinated Chicken Recipe

Description

Marinated Garlic Soy Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Minced Garlic

Steps

  1. Pour Light Soy Sauce into a Bowl
  2. Add Minced Garlic
  3. Add Chicken Thighs
  4. Ensure Chicken is Coated and Submerged
  5. Cover and Marinate for 2 Hours
  6. Heat Pan on High Heat
  7. Fry Chicken until Brown and Crispy
