Fragrant Rice Recipe

Description

Fragrant Jasmine Rice with Onion, Garlic and Spices.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Minced Garlic
  • Chopped Onion
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Cumin Powder
  • Chicken Bone Broth

Steps

  1. Wash Rice 2-3 times
  2. Heat Pot with Oil
  3. Fry Onion and Garlic
  4. Add in Washed Rice
  5. Add in Bone Broth and Powder
  6. Add in Water
  7. Lid off until Water/Broth is boiling
  8. Low Heat, lid on for 10-15 minutes
  9. Turn off and let it sit
